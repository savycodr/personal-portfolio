Heather is a full stack software engineer with background in architecture, engineering, and software development. Her education includes a M.S. in Mechanical Engineering, a B.A. in Physics, and a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering. In July 2019 she earns a cerificate from the full stack coding bootcamp at University of Pennsylvania. Experienced in Java, HTML, JavaScript, jQuery, Firebase, CSS, Bootstrap and Materialize.

She began her career designing airplanes and writing the software to model them. She was on the design team for the F-35C Joint Strike Fighter. She wrote software to aid in the design of the F-22 Raptor and the C-130J Hercules. Her designs were published in Aviation Week and Space Technology. Along the way, she developed a passion for software development.

Community-oriented, Heather is Vice President of an organization that fundraises and serves the community. She coordinates volunteer services at the homeless shelter. One day a week, she teaches a kindergarten class.
